About Me

As a recent graduate of Grand Valley State University with years of education in the theoretical and technical sides of cartography, as well as my research and analysis skills, I am well prepared for performing in a fast paced environment that requires critical thinking and project management.

My education has enabled me to not only manage multiple projects, but to also think creatively and critically for whatever problem I face. From my job at the library as a research consultant to my years at the farmers market in Holland, I have developed my interpersonal skills. Organizing my time and paying attention to detail is something I have honed through my research consultations, my secretary position at the nursing college, and my GIS internship with the Grand Rapids Parks and Recreation Department. Whether it was managing multiple mapping projects for the Parks and Recreation Department, or managing the schedules of code inspectors at the Code Compliance Division for Grand Rapids, my project management skills prevailed.
