About Me

As a graduate of Grand Valley State University with years of education in Geographic Information Systems and data analysis, I am looking to start a career in the world of GIS.

My education has enabled me to both visualize and analyze data through the ESRI software suite. Moreover, Grand Valley taught me to teach myself when I come against complex concepts.

Since graduation, I have been teaching myself to further explore within and without the ERSI ecosystem through programming. By learning Python and SQL, it has richly enabled my understanding of data analysis and databases. On top of that, this website is an accumulation of hours spent learning the ins-and-outs of web programming.

Going forward, I would love to apply my new-found skills in a professional setting. Please check out my portfolio below to see how I can be an asset to your team.
